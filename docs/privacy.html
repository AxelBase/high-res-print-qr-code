Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 23, 2025

Welcome to the High-Resolution Print QR Generator, a fully client-side, open-source web tool created by AxelBase. We are deeply committed to your privacy. This policy explains exactly how your information (or lack thereof) is handled when you use our tool.

1. Zero Data Collection — By Design

We do not collect, store, log, or transmit any data from your use of this tool. Everything happens directly in your browser. When you:

  • Type a URL, vCard, Wi-Fi credentials, or any content
  • Upload a logo image
  • Customize colors, corners, or error correction
  • Download PNG or SVG files

All processing occurs locally on your device. No information ever reaches our servers or any third party.

2. What This Means in Practice

Because the entire application runs client-side using JavaScript:

  • No cookies are set
  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting
  • No IP addresses are logged
  • No usage statistics are recorded
  • Your QR content (even sensitive data like Wi-Fi passwords or vCards) never leaves your computer

3. Local Storage & Temporary Data

The tool may temporarily use your browser’s memory to hold your current input during active use. This data is automatically cleared when you close the tab or refresh the page. We do not use localStorage or IndexedDB to persist any information.

4. Third-Party Libraries

We use only trusted, open-source libraries (qr-code-styling, Bootstrap via CDN) loaded from reputable sources. These do not track users or send data back to us.

5. Downloads & Sharing

When you download a PNG or SVG file, it is generated directly in your browser and saved to your device. We have no access to the resulting file or its contents.

6. Children’s Privacy

This tool is suitable for all ages. Since no personal information is collected, we fully comply with COPPA and similar regulations by default.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if new features are added. Any changes will be clearly dated at the top of this page.

8. Contact

Questions? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Your privacy is not a feature — it’s the foundation. Use this tool with complete confidence, knowing nothing is watched, stored, or shared.
